MINOR: BUSINESS STUDIES

SEMESTER 1
Course Code: MG1DSCBST100
Course Title: MARKETING MANAGEMENT
Course Type: DSC B
Credits: 4
Hours/Week: 5 (Lecture: 3, Practical: 2)

Course Summary:
Understanding marketing concepts, principles, evolving trends, and real-world marketing practices.

Course Outcomes:
1. Understand marketing concepts, opportunities, and challenges
2. Understand marketing environment and value chain
3. Apply segmentation, targeting, and positioning
4. Analyze product development strategies
5. Develop marketing strategies (Product, Price, Promotion, Place)
6. Understand marketing trends and strategy adaptation

Key Modules:
- Marketing concepts and evolution
- Marketing environment (Micro & Macro)
- Marketing 1.0 to 5.0
- Value Chain
- Marketing Mix
- STP (Segmentation, Targeting, Positioning)
- Product Life Cycle
- Branding, Packaging, Pricing
- Distribution, Logistics, Retailing
- Promotion Mix, Advertising, Sales Promotion
- Role Plays, Case Studies, Field Work

Assessment:
CCA: 30 Marks
End Semester Exam: 70 Marks (MCQs)

References:
- Sherlekar & Krishnamoorthy – Marketing Management
- Philip Kotler – Marketing Management
- Rajan Saxena – Marketing Management
